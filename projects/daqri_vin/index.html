What is VIN

VIN is one kind of SLAM (simultaneous localization and mapping) software that integrate a camera and an IMU (applicable to multiple cameras and IMUs if available), in order to achieve both 1) accurate, and 2) little delay that are both critical for Augmeneted Display.

VIN Hardware Setup

This setup consists of two major parts supporting the calibration of the smart helmet or smart glasses: 1) inside a robot cell with safety system, we have a kuka robot, calibration targets, fixtures, and 2) robot controller and calibration station outside the cell.
